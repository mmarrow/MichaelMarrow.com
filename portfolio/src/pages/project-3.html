What is Bright House Networks Small Business Month? We invite you to explore your community and support the many small businesses right in our backyard. With our new online directory, we've made it easier than ever to find your new local favorites. From restaurants to mechanics, there's something to find for everyone. Simply search using your zip code or find what category you're looking for.

At Bright House Networks, we believe in small businesses. That’s why we’re proud to team up with local Chambers of Commerce for the Regional Business Awards and Small Business Month to recognize their achievements and encourage customers to shop local and support small businesses.

This was a project with a very agressive deadline. The design of the site was done by a local agecny and handed over to me. I used foundation framework to quickly whip up a fully responsive layout. The design called for a parallax scroll effect which I tweaked a jQuery plugin to make it fit my needs for this project. We also integrated googlemaps API to allow customers to find businesses local to them. The core technologies used for this project were Foundation Framework, SASS, jQuery/JavaScript, Google Maps API all wrapped in a .NET MVC backend.
