So what is BHTV? BHTV is all about convenience, choice, and control. Enjoy even more of your Bright House Networks TV service anywhere you go. Our Video On Demand features some of your favorites such as AMC, E!, Nick Jr., and Univision. If you want to stream Live TV then you can watch channels such as News 13, Bay News 9, Bright House Sports Network, ESPN, Travel, and HGTV. In addition to watching TV on most wireless devices in and around your home, BHTV lets you watch Video On Demand and stream Live TV of some of your favorite channels anywhere you have Internet access. Available for Computer, iPad, iPhone, Android and Kindle Fire HD.

I was the lead Front-End Developer on the BHTV team. This was a large codebase that Bright House forked from Time Warner. My responsibilities included making UI/UX design and development. My day to day in this role mostly included CSS updates via a very robust SASS architecture as well as jQuery to handle user interations and events. The front-end was built with backbone.js framework which I also did some work on.
